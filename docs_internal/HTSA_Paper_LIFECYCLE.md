# HTSA_Paper lifecycle

- Canonical active trio: `HTSA_Paper.tex`, `HTSA_Paper.pdf`, and `HTSA_Paper.docx`.
- Historical one-off versions live under `HTSA_Paper_archive/historical_variants/`.
- LaTeX build byproducts for the paper live under `HTSA_Paper_archive/build_artifacts/` and can be regenerated from the canonical `.tex`.
- Do not create new top-level `HTSA_Paper_*` exports during drafting; replace the canonical trio and archive superseded variants instead.
- The official external sync target for the paper is `git@github.com:pzg8794/BIOL550-Project_Paper.git`.
- Delete archived variants only after the final synchronized paper version is confirmed.
